Executive Summary

DIA‑10 proposes a diamond‑inspired digital asset designed to combine a clear scarcity signal with modern, auditable token rails. The project targets practical utility (access control, governance experiments, staking) and a transparent brand narrative.

Founder Profile

Markus Goffin – visionary, strategist and innovator with 35+ years across jewelry & diamonds, brand building, and international trade.

Starting at Juwelier Walter Goffin (Düsseldorf), Markus led repositioning and launched innovations like the internationally patented “Ruby in the ring inner band®”. Long‑standing ties to Antwerp and Israel underpin a resilient global network.

He founded/grew MODEGO, AMORE DUE® and DORA®, managed CI/marketing for renowned brands, directed trade‑show campaigns, and introduced sales trainings that materially lifted profitability.

Continuous education spans diamondology (Antwerp), gemology (Idar‑Oberstein), goldsmithing, watchmaking, marketing, web design, and corporate consulting—enabling concept‑to‑market leadership.

With DIA‑10, Markus fuses real‑world value knowledge with blockchain tooling—aiming for a transparent, durable digital asset inspired by the scarcity and timelessness of diamonds.
